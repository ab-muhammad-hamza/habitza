## Features
- **Sub-habits**: Break habits into smaller sub-tasks; complete them one by one or all at once
- **Full year calendar**: See your entire year at a glance; tap past days to mark or review progress
- **Offline-first PWA**: Everything lives in your browser/indexedDB; installable, no account needed
- **Habit journal**: Attach notes/diary entries to any habit (migrated to a standalone system)
- **Minimalist UI**: Clean, card-based layout with compact or default inline calendar views
